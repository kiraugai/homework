ABOUT ME

My name is Kira Ugai and I was born in a small town in Russia in 1992. In 2000, my family made a big move to the Netherlands - where I lived for the most part of my life. During my 2nd year in college I founded my own e-commerce business - Beauty Millenium - specialized in beauty products. In order to find manufacturers to produce my merchandise, I traveled to countries such as Greece, Italy, China and Korea. Traveling abroad helped me to acquire a better understanding of different cultures and improved my ability to visualize ideas and convert them into real-life projects.

Last year I graduated from the University of Amsterdam, with a bachelors degree in Communication Science. I decided it was time for a new adventure so I packed my bags and moved to San Francisco, CA - where I am currently residing! Here in SF, I hope to make new connections, gain new skills (such as coding) and collaborate with new brands in order to expand my business further.
